- Custom PCB
- Custom case (Will probably be 3D printed)
- Touchscreen
- Wifi + Bluetooth capabilities
- USB-C port
- Side buttons
- Accelerometer
- Gyroscope
- Magnetometer
- Rechargable battery
- ~64 MB of storage
- App system
